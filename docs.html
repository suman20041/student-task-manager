🚀 Getting Started

Simply enter your task in the input box, select a priority, choose a due date, and click Add.

🔍 Search & Filtering

Use the search bar to quickly find tasks. The list filters in real-time as you type.

✏️ Editing Tasks

Click the Edit button on any task to change its text inline. Click Save to commit your changes.

📊 Progress Tracking

The progress bar at the bottom updates automatically as you complete tasks. Finish all tasks to trigger a celebration!

🎨 Themes

Switch between Light, Dark, Blue, and Pastel themes using the dropdown in the header. Your preference is saved automatically.
