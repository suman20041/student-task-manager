🔒 Privacy Policy

👋 Focus Time!

Status 🔒 Secure

1. Information We Collect

TaskQuest is a local-first application. We do not collect or store any of your personal data on external servers. All task data, XP, coins, and settings are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

2. How We Use Data

Your gamification data is used solely to provide the productivity features of this application. Since the data remains on your device, we have no access to your study schedules or completion status.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Chart.js for analytics visualizations and jsPDF for exporting your data locally. These libraries operate entirely on the client-side. No tracking scripts or cookies are used.

4. Data Security

Your data is as secure as your browser and device. Clearing your browser cache or site data will remove your progress permanently unless you use the Export feature.
