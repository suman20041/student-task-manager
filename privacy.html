🛡️ Privacy Policy

🏠 Home

1. Information We Collect

The Study Task Tracker is a local-first application. We do not collect or store any of your personal data on our servers. All task data is stored directly in your browser's localStorage.

2. How We Use Data

Your task data is used solely to provide the task management features of this application. Since the data remains on your device, we have no access to your study schedules, priorities, or completion status.

3. Third-Party Services

We use canvas-confetti for celebratory animations, which is loaded via a CDN. No tracking scripts or cookies are used by this application.

4. Data Security

Your data is as secure as your browser and device. Clearing your browser cache or site data will remove your tasks permanently.
