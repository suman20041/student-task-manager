# Contributing to Nexus Task Master

We love your contributions! To maintain the premium quality of this project, please follow these guidelines:

## 🚀 How to Contribute

1.  **Fork** the repository.
2.  **Clone** your fork to your local machine.
3.  **Create a new branch** for your feature or fix: `git checkout -b feature/amazing-feature`.
4.  **Implement your changes** following the project's design system (Glassmorphism, Outfit font).
5.  **Test your changes** to ensure responsiveness and functionality.
6.  **Commit** your changes with clear, descriptive messages.
7.  **Push** to your fork and **Open a Pull Request**.

## 🎨 Design Guidelines

- Use CSS variables defined in `style.css` for colors and spacing.
- Ensure all new components are responsive.
- Maintain the glassmorphism aesthetic.
