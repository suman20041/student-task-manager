    Productivity Tips

    Avoiding Distractions

    Create a dedicated study space, silence notifications, and use focused intervals (25–50 mins) followed by short breaks.

    Effective Study Methods

    Use active recall and spaced repetition. Teach back concepts to check true understanding.

    Time Management

    Prioritize tasks with an impact/effort matrix and block peak-focus time for deep work.

    Focus Improvement

    Train attention with single-task sessions, remove multi-tasking, and practice brief mindfulness before starting.

    Consistency Habits

    Build small daily rituals (study start routine, review, and reflection) to make progress automatic.
